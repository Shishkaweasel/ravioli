About Us

Premier Roofing is a family-run business established in 1990 by Andrew and Deborah Lamothe. Although the company has since grown and evolved, Andrew still personally oversees all aspects of every project the company undertakes. Premier Roofing continues to tailor its work in order to best meet the unique needs of all our customers.

Our Certifications

Premier Roofing holds a mandatory Régie du bâtiment du Québec (RBQ) permit; our permit is a specialized roofing contractor's permit and was earned after passing all required RBQ examinations. We are fully bonded and have $2,000,000.00 of liability insurance.

Premier Roofing is one of a few elite companies certified Platinum by BP Roofing Products of Canada to install their shingles. We are also a certified CertainTeed installer.

Installing a roof is more than just nailing on shingles, and you can rest easy knowing that we are fully qualified to do the job right.
